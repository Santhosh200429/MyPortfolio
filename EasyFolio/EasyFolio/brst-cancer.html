AI-Powered Breast Cancer Prediction

This project leverages machine learning to predict the presence of breast cancer with high accuracy. By analyzing medical data, our AI model helps in early detection and diagnosis.

We used advanced algorithms such as Random Forest, SVM, and Neural Networks to achieve reliable predictions based on real-world medical datasets.

Early detection saves lives. AI is transforming healthcare with accurate predictions

Our model was trained on the Wisconsin Breast Cancer Dataset (WBCD) and optimized for high precision and recall scores, ensuring minimal false negatives in predictions.
